# KuryenteKo — Demo Video Script

> **Target length:** 90 seconds – 2 minutes
> **Format:** Screen recording with voiceover
> **Tone:** Conversational, confident, Taglish-friendly
>
> Each scene lists the `[SCREEN]` direction (what to show on device) followed by the voiceover line.

---

## Scene 1 — Hook (0:00 – 0:10)

**[SCREEN]** Home screen of KuryenteKo. Show the app name, the orange KoKo owl mascot, and the two main action buttons.

> *"Alam mo ba kung tama ang bayad mo sa kuryente? Most Filipinos have questioned their electric bill at least once — wondering if the numbers add up, or if what they're paying is even within the legal ERC rate. KuryenteKo finally gives you the answer."*

---

## Scene 2 — OCR Bill Scanner (0:10 – 0:28)

**[SCREEN]** Tap "I-scan ang Bill Ko" → scanner screen opens → tap the large dark camera card → live camera viewfinder appears with the yellow alignment frame corners → hold a printed bill up to the frame → tap the yellow shutter button → photo preview screen appears ("Suriin ang larawan") → tap "Gamitin Ito."

> *"Just point your camera at your Meralco bill. KuryenteKo reads only the billing numbers — not your name, not your account number, nothing personal. Tap 'Gamitin Ito' and the AI gets to work."*

**[SCREEN]** Scanning spinner: "Binabasa ang bill mo..."

> *"In seconds, our AI extracts every charge from your bill."*

---

## Scene 3 — Confirm & Edit (0:28 – 0:36)

**[SCREEN]** Confirm screen ("Tama ba ito?") — show the extracted values with yellow-border highlights on Total Amount, kWh, and Rate per kWh. Scroll briefly to show the individual charge fields below (Generation, Transmission, etc.).

> *"You see everything it found — and you can edit any field before analysis. Full control, full transparency."*

---

## Scene 4 — Bill Decoder (0:36 – 0:50)

**[SCREEN]** Bill Decoder screen. Show the dark navy summary card at the top — total bill in large white text, kWh chip, rate chip, city chip (all in yellow). Scroll down to the charge breakdown list. Tap one charge row to expand it — show the Taglish explanation text and the green/amber/red status badge.

> *"The Bill Decoder breaks down every peso of your bill — Generation, Transmission, Distribution — and flags anything that's too high based on ERC-official rates. Each row has a plain-language explanation of exactly what you're paying for."*

---

## Scene 5 — Verdict / Overcharge Result (0:50 – 1:07)

**[SCREEN]** Tap "TINGNAN ANG RESULTA ⚡". Verdict screen animates in. If overcharged: the red card appears with "May Overcharge!" and the peso overcharge amount in large bold text. Hold here for 2 seconds. Then scroll down slowly to show the Rate Comparison table (user rate vs ERC maximum vs difference) and the "Mga Pwedeng Gawin" action buttons.

> *"KuryenteKo gives you a verdict. If your effective rate exceeds the ERC legal maximum, it tells you exactly how much you were overcharged — down to the centavo. It also explains why rates are high right now: El Niño demand spikes, the decline of Malampaya gas supply, WESM spot price surges — all linked to real news articles you can read."*

---

## Scene 6 — ERC Complaint with Legal Basis & Template (1:07 – 1:18)

**[SCREEN]** From the Verdict screen, tap "Gumawa ng ERC Complaint ⚖️". ERC Complaint screen opens. Show the auto-generated complaint message template pre-filled with the user's actual bill details — city, total amount, kWh, rate, overcharge amount, and the legal citation. Scroll briefly to show the "Kailan Pwede Magreklamo?" grounds list and the 4-step filing guide. Show the tappable ERC email/hotline contacts at the bottom.

> *"Hit the ERC Complaint button and KuryenteKo drafts a ready-to-send formal complaint — pre-filled with your bill data, the exact overcharge amount, and the specific ERC rule that was violated. You get the grounds for your complaint, the legal basis, a step-by-step filing guide, and direct one-tap contact links to the ERC and Meralco. No legal background needed."*

---

## Scene 7 — Community Heat Map (1:18 – 1:27)

**[SCREEN]** Tap "Tingnan ang Area Map" from the Verdict screen or tap the Map tab. Full-screen map loads with colored pins across Metro Manila and other Philippine cities — green (fair), yellow (elevated), red (overcharged). Hold on the map for 2 seconds, then switch to the list view and show per-city average rates and report counts.

> *"See where overcharging is happening across the Philippines. Every pin is a real community report. You're not alone — and now there's a record."*

---

## Scene 8 — KoKo AI Chatbot (1:27 – 1:39)

**[SCREEN]** Tap "Magtanong sa KoKo AI 🤖". Chat screen opens with KoKo's owl avatar. Tap the quick prompt chip "Bakit mataas ang bill ko?" — KoKo responds with a formatted Taglish answer referencing El Niño or WESM spot prices. Scroll to show the source cards at the bottom with Inquirer and BusinessWorld links.

> *"Still have questions? Ask KoKo — your personal electricity bill assistant. KoKo knows your current bill, your rate, your city, and the real reasons behind every peso increase. It cites real news sources — not guesswork."*

---

## Scene 9 — Personalized Bill History (1:39 – 1:47)

**[SCREEN]** Switch to the History tab. Show 2–3 past bill cards — each with the billing month, city, total amount, and a verdict badge (green "Normal" or red "Overcharged"). Tap one card to re-open the full Verdict screen for that bill.

> *"Every bill you analyze is saved to your personal history. Track your charges month over month, spot trends, and go back to any past verdict anytime."*

---

## Scene 10 — Home Dashboard (1:47 – 2:02)

**[SCREEN]** Return to the Home screen. Show the dashboard widgets:
- **"Estimatong Bill Ngayong Buwan"** card with a peso estimate and "Batay sa iyong bill noong [last month]" label
- **"Fairness Check"** card in red/amber/green with user rate vs ERC maximum
- **"Kommunity Update"** showing the live bill-verified count and a bar chart of the most overcharged cities

> *"And on your home screen: a real-time dashboard. Your estimated bill for this month, calculated from your actual past consumption. A Fairness Check that instantly tells you if your current rate is legal. And live community data — how many bills have been verified nationwide, and which cities are being hit hardest. KuryenteKo isn't just a scanner. It's your electricity watchdog."*

---

## Scene 11 — Closing (2:02 – 2:08)

**[SCREEN]** Hold on the KuryenteKo home screen with the KoKo owl mascot. Fade to app icon.

> *"KuryenteKo. Para sa bawat Pilipinong may karapatang malaman kung tama ang kanilang bill. ⚡"*

---

## Director's Notes

| Scene | Key thing to show clearly | Watch out for |
|-------|--------------------------|---------------|
| 2 — Scanner | Camera aligning with a real **printed** bill, not a phone screen | Glare from lamp — scan at an angle |
| 5 — Verdict | Peso overcharge in large red text — **this is the hero moment** | Make sure demo bill rate is above ₱14.3496/kWh |
| 6 — ERC | The pre-filled complaint template and the ERC Rule citation | Scroll slowly so judges can read the grounds |
| 7 — Map | Switch between map and list view | Wait for map tiles to load before recording |
| 8 — KoKo | Wait for the full AI response to finish generating before cutting | Use a short question so generation is fast |
| 10 — Dashboard | If Phase 8 widgets are not yet live, cut this to 8 seconds and name the feature verbally | Do not show empty/zero widgets |

> **Pre-recording tip:** Run through the full OCR scan with a real bill before recording. Use a bill where the effective rate is slightly above ₱14.35/kWh so the verdict shows "May Overcharge" — the red result card is far more visually compelling than green for a hackathon audience.
