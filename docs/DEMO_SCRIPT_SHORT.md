# KuryenteKo — 60-Second Demo Script

> **Target length:** ~60 seconds
> **Format:** Screen recording with voiceover
> **Tone:** Punchy, confident, Taglish-friendly

---

## Scene 1 — Hook (0:00 – 0:07)

**[SCREEN]** Home screen — KoKo owl mascot, app name, two main action buttons.

> *"Tama ba ang bayad mo sa kuryente? Most Filipinos question their bill — but nobody checks. KuryenteKo does."*

---

## Scene 2 — Scan & Decode (0:07 – 0:25)

**[SCREEN]** Tap "I-scan ang Bill Ko" → camera opens → align printed bill to yellow frame → tap shutter → "Binabasa ang bill mo…" spinner → Bill Decoder screen loads. Scroll through the charge breakdown — tap one row to expand the Taglish explanation + status badge.

> *"Just point your camera at your Meralco bill. In seconds, every charge is decoded — Generation, Transmission, Distribution — each one flagged against the official ERC rate. Plain language, no accounting degree needed."*

---

## Scene 3 — Verdict (0:25 – 0:40)

**[SCREEN]** Tap "TINGNAN ANG RESULTA ⚡" → red "May Overcharge!" card appears with the peso amount in large text. Hold 2 seconds. Scroll to show the Rate Comparison table, then the El Niño alert banner, then tap one issue accordion (e.g. "El Niño at Summer Peak Demand") to expand it. Tap a source card to show it links to a real news article.

> *"The verdict is instant. If your rate exceeds the ERC legal maximum, KuryenteKo tells you exactly how much you were overcharged — and why: El Niño, Malampaya decline, WESM price spikes. Every claim linked to a real news source."*

---

## Scene 4 — ERC Complaint (0:40 – 0:50)

**[SCREEN]** Tap "Gumawa ng ERC Complaint ⚖️" → pre-filled complaint template appears with bill data, overcharge amount, and ERC Rule citation. Tap the "I-Email sa ERC" button.

> *"One tap generates a ready-to-send formal complaint — pre-filled with your data, your overcharge amount, and the exact ERC rule violated. No lawyer needed."*

---

## Scene 5 — KoKo AI (0:50 – 0:57)

**[SCREEN]** Chat screen — tap a quick prompt chip "Bakit mataas ang bill ko?" → KoKo responds with a formatted Taglish answer, source cards visible at the bottom.

> *"Still have questions? Ask KoKo AI — your personal electricity assistant. Real answers. Real sources."*

---

## Scene 6 — Closing (0:57 – 1:02)

**[SCREEN]** Hold on home screen with the KoKo owl. Fade to app icon.

> *"KuryenteKo. Para sa bawat Pilipinong may karapatang malaman kung tama ang kanilang bill. ⚡"*

---
